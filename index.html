Catch the Ramen

<DOES NOT CURRENTLY SUPPORT MOBILE DEVICES>


Demonstration

Instructions

You are Naruto, and your goal is to catch all of the ramen as quickly as possible

Controls

  • Difficulty: Use the difficulty buttons to select the desired difficulty

  • Motion: The player's movement may be controlled with the arrow keys

  • Catching: The catching action may be performed by pressing the spacebar. The action will only be successful if a bowl of ramen is close enough the player
